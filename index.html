Boston Hub

The Boston Hub is our enterprise and partnership office in Massachusetts. Dedicated to accelerating the translation of global-world impact, the Hub is a front door for biotech, tech, and industry partners seeking to collaborate with Monash University's globally recognised research ecosystem.
